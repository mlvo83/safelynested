Onboarding a Charity

A guide for charity staff joining the SafelyNested platform
What this guide covers. Every step a charity needs to take to join SafelyNested — from submitting your application, to receiving approval, to creating the primary contact account that runs your charity on the platform. This document is for the person at your organization who will lead the onboarding.

Before you begin

Joining SafelyNested takes about a week from application to active account. The process is:

  1. You fill out a public application form on SafelyNested.
  2. A SafelyNested administrator reviews your application.
  3. If approved, your designated primary contact receives an email link to create their account.
  4. The primary contact sets up their username and password and logs in.

You do not need a SafelyNested account to apply. The application is open to the public.

The 6-step onboarding flow

1

Submit your application

Open the application page in any browser:

safelynested.com/charity-application/apply

Fill out the form in full. Take your time on the description and mission statement — this is what the reviewer reads.

At the bottom, check the box agreeing to the SafelyNested Terms and Conditions, then click Submit Application.

Important: Only one pending application per email address is allowed. If you submit and then realize something needs to change, contact SafelyNested support rather than trying to submit again.
2

Receive your confirmation email

Within a few minutes you should receive an email titled:

Application Received: CA-YYYY-NNNN - SafelyNested

That code — CA-YYYY-NNNN — is your application number. Save it. You'll need it to check your status.

Tip: If the email doesn't arrive within 15 minutes, check your spam or junk folder before contacting support.
3

(Optional) Check your application status anytime

If you want to confirm your application is in the queue, visit:

safelynested.com/charity-application/status

Enter your application number and the email you applied with. The status will be one of:

  • Pending — received, awaiting review.
  • Under Review — an administrator is actively looking at it.
  • Approved — you should also have received the approval email (see Step 4).
  • Rejected — an explanation will be in the rejection email.
4

Receive your approval email

Once a SafelyNested administrator approves your application, the primary contact will receive an email titled:

Application Approved: CA-YYYY-NNNN - SafelyNested

The email contains a Create Your Account button. This button is unique to your charity and takes you to the registration page.

Time-sensitive: The Create Your Account link expires 7 days after approval. If it expires before you finish, you'll need to contact SafelyNested support to issue a new link — the original cannot be reused.
5

Create the primary contact account

Click the Create Your Account button in the approval email. You'll land on a registration form pre-filled with your email address (read-only).

Fill in:

  • Username * — what you'll type when you log in. Cannot be changed later without admin help, so choose carefully.
  • Password * — pick a strong password (12+ characters, mix of letters, numbers, symbols recommended).
  • Confirm Password * — type it again to catch typos.
  • First name and last name — optional but recommended; appears across the platform.
  • Phone — optional.
  • Agree to the Terms and Conditions * — required.

Click Create Account. You'll be redirected to the login page with a success message.

Behind the scenes: Two important things happen automatically when you complete this step:
  • Your account is set as the charity's primary contact. Only the primary contact can manage the team.
  • The domain of your email (for example, shelterorg.com) is locked in as the allowed team email domain. From now on, any team member you invite must have an email at the same domain. This prevents accidental invitations to outside parties.
6

Log in and explore your dashboard

You'll receive a welcome email titled:

Welcome to SafelyNested - {Your Charity Name}

Click Log In Now in the email, or go to safelynested.com/login and sign in with the username and password you just created.

You'll land on your Charity Partner Dashboard. From here you can:

  • Create referrals for families needing housing.
  • Send invite links to participants.
  • Manage your charity's locations.
  • Set up donors (see the separate Charity Donor Onboarding guide).
  • Invite team members and (after admin promotion) facilitators — see the separate Charity Facilitator Onboarding guide.

What if your application is rejected?

If a reviewer determines your organization isn't a fit, you'll receive an email titled Application Update: CA-YYYY-NNNN - SafelyNested. The email contains a written reason. You're welcome to address the reason and submit a new application, or contact SafelyNested directly to discuss.

Help and support

If you run into any issue at any step — an email didn't arrive, a link expired, the form errored, anything — reach out to your SafelyNested administrator. Because this platform helps families find safe shelter, we treat onboarding issues as urgent.
